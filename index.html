“

We at TMS feel that the Middle East is rich with talented musicians. They are passionately honing their skills, innovating and evolving their music, and dreaming of a global audience. We wanted a space where we could enjoy watching an amazing local band, talented beyond our expectations. And we are so happy for them. For their victory! And we are all so close to them. We can talk to them after the show –sometimes during! We wanted a space that seated 100. Maximum 120! Big enough to be a crowd, but small enough to connect with the Musicians. And then we did some CRAZY SPENDING. We got a KICKASS sound system with way too many speakers! We got an amazing lighting system. We got a GIGANTIC LED screen and got a DJ and VJ onboard. We made a cool stage and DJ stand. We wanted a place where Musicians loved to play. And we wanted lovers of alternative music to call TMS the best place to be for music!
